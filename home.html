I.C Nagoya introduction

35 years experience

I.C Nagoya has been established and teaching Japanese for more than 35 years. We leverage that experience and continuously improve our teaching methods, to make sure you get the best out of your time studying with us.

Location

I.C Nagoya is located right next to the Nagoya station. Nagoya station is one of the largest train station in the world. This means you can easily travel all over Japan, using Nagoya as your base. And not matter where your accommodation is, it will be easy to commute to the school.

Activites

I.C Nagoya regularly organizes off-campus learning and exciting events. Studying is very important, but what best, to learn a language, than to also immerse yourself in the local culture, and enjoy all it has to offer!

EJU

I.C Nagoya will prepare you for the EJU (Examination for Japanese University), and admission exam for international students. Because you have goals for your future, and we know that this could be a life-changing experience.

Job hunt

I.C Nagoya is one of the few insitutions selected by the Japanese government to allow its students to stay in Japan to job hunt for up to 1 year after their graduation. Because choosing the right job should not be done in a hurry.

Support

Thanks to our 35-year-experience, we are able to provide academic as well as life support to the students that join us. Need help with settling in Japan? Want to organize a trip and need advices? We got you covered!
