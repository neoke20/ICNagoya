1-year course with a student visa

Our 1 year student visa program consists of 40 weeks of our Comprehensive Japanese course divided in terms of 10 weeks each and a total of 9 Japanese levels starting from JLPT 5 level. 1 level is completed every 3 months. 20 lessons per week, 4 a day, are provided Monday to Friday in either the morning or afternoon depending on the level of the student. Students can extend their stay to study for up to two years.

Who is it for?

- Students who want to study in a university, or continue higher education in Japan

- Students who aim to work in Japanese companies.

- Students who want to use proper Japanese in their daily life.

- Students who aim to improve their Japanese ability.

How we teach

Students learn Japanese comprehensively, as a means of communication, rather than just academically.
