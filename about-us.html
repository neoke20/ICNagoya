Our school

The “I.C.” of I.C.NAGOYA stands for “International Communication”. We were the first Japanese language school in the Chubu area to be authorized by the Ministry of Justice to accept international students while providing student visas.

We started welcoming students in 1983, and since then we have taken in learners from more than 45 countries.

Our teachers use their experience to guide and help our students enhance their communication skills. We help not by just teaching Japanese, but also by supplying our students with skills to find success in Japan. We offer guidance for those who want to enter a university or college, and we also help those of our students who dream of finding employment.

Living in another country can be difficult, so we help our students to navigate through laws and regulations that they may have to face so that they can concentrate on their studies. Our graduates have gone to universities, vocational schools and graduate schools, some have found employment in Japan, while others chose to return to their home countries to work for Japanese companies, or as translators and interpreters.

We make every decision with the following idea in mind: “We teach people to think and act from a global point of view”.

Nagoya is an affordable city, compared to other big cities worldwide. It is an urban centre, but with beautiful parks and historical spots. It is a great place to live and concentrate on Japanese study!
